# People Analytics Case 3 – Engagement

Understanding the key drivers of employee engagement and translating insights into strategic HR decisions.

---

## Objective

The company aims to better understand employee engagement by answering key business questions:

- What factors explain engagement levels  
- Is salary a real driver of engagement  
- Are there critical moments in the employee lifecycle  
- Does engagement impact performance  

The goal is to transform these insights into actionable HR strategies.

---

## Hypotheses

The analysis is based on the following hypotheses:

- Leadership (Manager Rating) is the main driver of engagement  
- Work-Life Balance has a significant impact  
- Salary has limited influence on engagement  
- There is an engagement decline between 2–4 years of tenure  
- Higher engagement leads to better performance  

---

## Dataset

The dataset includes key HR variables such as:

- Engagement score  
- Performance score  
- Salary  
- Tenure (years in the company)  
- Manager rating  
- Work-life balance  

These variables allow analyzing both behavioral and structural drivers of engagement.

---

## Methodology

- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Correlation analysis  
- Segmentation by tenure  
- Hypothesis validation  
- Visualization of engagement patterns  

---

## Key Insights

(To be completed after analysis)

- Manager rating shows strong correlation with engagement  
- Salary impact is weaker than expected  
- Engagement declines between years 2–4  
- Engagement positively correlates with performance  

---

## Business Implications

The results enable HR to:

- Improve leadership effectiveness  
- Redesign employee experience at critical tenure stages  
- Prioritize non-monetary engagement drivers  
- Align engagement strategies with performance outcomes  

---

## Applications

- Engagement dashboards (Power BI)  
- Early detection of disengagement  
- Leadership development initiatives  
- Employee lifecycle optimization  

---

## Next Steps

- Develop a predictive engagement model  
- Integrate insights into HR analytics tools  
- Link engagement to business KPIs  

---

## Conclusion

This case shows how engagement can be treated as a strategic variable, enabling more precise and data-driven HR decisions.
